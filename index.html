I am a PhD student in Informatics at Indiana University, where my work sits at the intersection of computing, culture, and society.

My research explores how sociotechnical systems shape participation, power, and lived experience, with a particular interest in design practices and values.

Through my work, I aim to contribute to more reflective, equitable, and human-centered approaches to technology design and evaluation.
